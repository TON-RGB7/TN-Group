Privacy Policy

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

a) TN Group respects the privacy of every individual who visits www.tn-grp.com. This policy explains our online information practices. Because we are respectful of your privacy, we provide you with notice and policy about the collection and use of your information.

b) References to "TN Group", "we", "us" and "our" are references to TN Group Of Companies, its affiliates and staff.

c) Hereafter references to “Website” refer specifically to TN Group's corporate website www.tn-grp.com.

d) This Policy should be read in conjunction with TN Group's Website Usage Terms & Conditions, also posted on this Website.

2. User consent

a) By using this Website or providing personal information to us, you are consenting to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal contact information as described in this Policy and any applicable Country or Web Site Privacy Statements.

b) If you do not consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal contact information as described in this Policy (and any applicable Country or Web Site Privacy Statements), do not use this Website or the “Contact TN Group” / “Contact Us” forms.

3. Browsing

a) This Website does not collect personally identifiable information when you browse this Website and request pages from our servers unless you voluntarily and knowingly provide it to us. This means that we will not know your name, your email address, or any other personally identifiable information just because you browse the Website.

b) When you request a page from our Website, our servers may automatically log the information provided in the HTTP request header, including the IP number, the time of the request, the URL of your request and other information that is provided in the HTTP header.

c) We collect the HTTP request header information in order to make our Website function correctly and provide you the functionality that you see on this Website. We may also use this information to personalize content presented to you; better understand how visitors browse our Website; and how we can better tune it, its contents, and functionality to meet your needs.

4. Information You Knowingly and Voluntarily Provide

a) We do not collect personally identifiable information from you unless you voluntarily and knowingly provide it to us when you use the Website, for example, the information you provide when you sign up to receive email news alerts, request a brochure or when you ask us a question. In many cases the information you provide will be personal contact information.

b) TN Group will use this information for the purposes for which you provide it. For example, the e-mail address provided when you sign up to receive e-mail news will be the e-mail address we use to deliver the e-mail news you request.

c) We may also use the information we collect on this Website for various business purposes such as customer service; fraud prevention; market research; improving our products and services; and providing you and your company with information and offers we believe may be of interest to you.

d) We may also remove all the personally identifiable information and use the rest for historical, statistical or scientific purposes.

5. Sharing Personal Contact Information

a) TN Group will not sell personal contact information collected from this Website to mailing list brokers without your express consent.

b) We may share personal contact information with other TN Group Of Companies business units. When we do so, these other TN Group Of Companies business units will solely use such information in a manner consistent with the purposes for which it was originally collected (or to which you subsequently consented) and only as permitted under this Policy, any applicable Country or Web Site Privacy Statements, and all applicable privacy and data protection laws.

c) We may also share personal contact information with third parties we hire to perform support services for us. These third parties are required to use the personal contact information we share with them only to perform services on our behalf and to treat personal contact information as strictly confidential.

d) In some cases, we may share personal contact information with third parties who partner with us to provide products and services to our customers. When we do so, we will require our business partners to use the personal contact information we share with them in a manner consistent with the purposes for which it was originally collected (or to which you subsequently consented) and only as permitted under this Policy, any applicable Country or Web Site Privacy Statements, and all applicable privacy and data protection laws.

e) In certain, limited circumstances we may share or transfer personal contact information to unrelated third parties. For example, we may provide personal contact information to a third party (i) at your request; (ii) to comply with a legal requirement or court order; (iii) to investigate a possible crime, such as identity theft; (iv) in connection with the sale, purchase, merger, reorganization, liquidation or dissolution of TN Group or a TN Group business unit; or (v) under similar circumstances. If such an event occurs, we will take appropriate steps to protect personal contact information.

6. Security of Personal Contact Information

a) Your personal contact information will generally be stored in TN Group databases or databases maintained by our service providers.

b) TN Group maintains reasonable safeguards to protect the confidentiality, security and integrity of your personal contact information. For example, we use secure socket layer (SSL) technology to transfer personal contact information over the Internet.

c) Although we use security measures to help protect your personal contact information against unauthorized disclosure, misuse or alteration, as is the case with all computer networks linked to the Internet, we cannot guarantee the security of information provided over the Internet and will not be responsible for breaches of security beyond our reasonable control.

7. Use of Personal Contact Information

a) If you provide TN Group with your personal contact information, either by a Website contact form or otherwise, we may enter your name into our constituent database and contact you in order to:
- Complete voluntary surveys seeking feedback for quality and service improvement purposes.
- Supply you with information including TN Group's construction projects, business news, and information on company expansion.
- Inform on the progression, development and news of other TN Group business units
- Offer seasonal greetings
- Distribute corporate messages

b) We may collect and record the email addresses of those who communicate with us by email and treat such personal contact information in line with this Policy.

c) General enquiries may be forwarded to the appropriate TN Group department for response and may be entered into our constituent database. If your name is entered into the database, we may include your details in correspondences as mentioned above.

a) TN Group's Website may contain links to Internet sites that are not operated by TN Group.

b) These links are provided as a service and do not imply any endorsement of the activities or content of these sites, nor any association with their operators.

c) TN Group does not control these Internet sites and is not responsible for their content, security, or privacy practices. We urge you to review the privacy policy posted on websites you visit before using the site or providing personal information.

9. Ongoing Development

a) This privacy statement may be updated periodically and re-posted on our Website. It applies only to our on-line practices and does not encompass other areas of the TN Group Of Companies.

b) We reserve the right to change this policy at any time by posting revisions, which will always be covered by clause (2.a) above.
